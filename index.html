Who Am I?

I am a west coast girl, open spaces and mountain ranges make me happy. I love people especially the ones who are insanely passionate about what they do. I’m a designer, explorer, connector and athlete. I’m crazy about life and plan to live a remarkable one. So far the experience has been pretty awesome.

What am I doing?

I’m doing what I’m good at. Math and science didn’t make sense but negative space did. Typography and photography are the elements I most love to work with, but ultimately I want to tell stories and help to create experiences.

Why am I doing it?

Because design is about people. People who love to create, to live, to explore. People who are passionate about life, beauty and curiosity. These are the people I want to work with, learn from and inspire.

Randomn Facts
  • I was homeschooled (till gr. 8)
  • The Tower of Terror actually terrifies me
  • I used to pronounce choir as "chore"
  • I have a weakness for musicals
